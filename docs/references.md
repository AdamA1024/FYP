# References

Key literature consulted during the project. Full citations appear in the
thesis bibliography; titles are listed here for orientation.

- *A Semi-Implicit Parallel Leapfrog Solver With Half-Step Sampling
  Technique for FPGA-Based Real-Time HIL Simulation of Power Converters* —
  basis for the half-step (leapfrog/Verlet) state update used by the twins.
- *FPGA-Based Digital Twin Implementation for Power Converter System
  Monitoring* — digital-twin framing and monitoring use case.
- *FPGA-Based Submicrosecond-Level Real-Time Simulation of Solid-State
  Transformer With a Switching Frequency of 50 kHz* — precedent for
  sub-microsecond real-time converter simulation on FPGA.
- *FPGA-Based Real-Time Simulation of Power Converters* — survey of
  real-time converter simulation architectures.
- *How Switching Frequency Affects a Buck Converter* (Texas Instruments
  application note) — buck design trade-offs used to pick test parameters.
- *Review and Outlook on GaN and SiC Power Devices* — context for the
  switching frequencies targeted by the DAB twin.
